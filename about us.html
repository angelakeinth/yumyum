About Us

Learn More About Us

Welcome to YUMYUM, where every dish tells a story. Founded in 2022, YUMYUM has been a cherished culinary destination in Sto. Tomas, Intramuros, Manil for 3 years. It all began with a vision to create a dining experience that celebrates not just great food, but also the rich tapestry of flavors, cultures, and traditions that inspire our menu.

Our journey started with a passion for authentic cuisine and a commitment to sourcing the finest, freshest ingredients. From humble beginnings in a cozy kitchen, we quickly gained a reputation for our innovative dishes that marry classic recipes with modern flair. Over the years, we've had the privilege of serving generations of loyal patrons, each sharing in our love for exceptional food and warm hospitality. Whether it's a romantic dinner for two or a lively gathering of friends and family, YUMYUM has always been a place where memories are made and stories are shared over plates of culinary delight.

Today, as we continue to evolve and grow, our commitment to quality and excellence remains unwavering. We invite you to join us on this gastronomic journey, where every visit promises a new chapter in our ongoing story of passion, flavor, and community. Thank you for being a part of the YUMYUM family. We look forward to creating unforgettable moments with you, one delicious dish at a time.
